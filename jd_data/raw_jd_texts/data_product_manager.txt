Job Title: Data Product Manager
Location: Hybrid – Boston / Remote
Experience Required: 2–5 Years
Role Overview:
We are looking for a Data Product Manager to lead strategy, development, and delivery of data-centric products. You will work closely with engineers, analysts, and stakeholders to turn business needs into actionable data solutions.
Responsibilities:
Define data product vision, roadmap, and KPIs
Collaborate with data engineers and analysts to deliver dashboards, APIs, and models
Own backlog and translate business problems into technical requirements
Lead adoption and user enablement for new data solutions
Skills Required:
2+ years in product management, preferably in a data-heavy role
Strong understanding of data pipelines, analytics, and visualization tools
Experience with tools like SQL, Looker, Tableau, or dbt
Clear communication skills to interface with both technical and business teams
